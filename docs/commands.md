Commands reference

/parlay
- Options: --legs, --mode (safe|normal|moonshot), --markets, --window, --game_time
- Output: multi-leg picks with confidence color, explanation, and aggregated risk label.

/pickoftheday
- Returns top 5 picks sorted by confidence and constrained to odds no worse than -200.

Feedback endpoints (CLI & Discord)
- feedback --id PICK_ID --result hit|miss
